Almond Croissant

A delicate pastry filled with the sweet nutty goodness of almond paste, dusted with powdered sugar, and baked to a golden perfection.

×
  1. 1 Sheet of puff pastry.
  2. 1/2 cup of almond paste.
  3. 1/4 cup of powdered sugar.
  4. 1 egg (for egg wash).
×

First, preheat the oven to 375°F. Next, roll out the puff pastry and cut it into triangles. Spread a thin layer of almond paste on each triangle.

Result:

Then, roll each triangle from the base to the top and curve into a crescent shape. Finally, brush with egg wash and sprinkle with powdered sugar, and bake for 15-20 minutes until it's golden brown.

Sourdough Bread

A community favorite, boasting a crusty exterior and a soft, airy interior, embodying the classic sourdough charm.

×
  1. 1 cup sourdough starter
  2. 1 1/2 cups warm water
  3. 4 cups bread flour
  4. 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
×

First, mix the starter, water, and 3 cups of flour in a large bowl. This should create a sticky dough. Next, add salt, and remaining flour 1 tablespoon at a time until fully incorporated. Then, knead the dough on a floured surface for about 15 minutes.

Result:

Once kneading is done, place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for about 5 hours. Finally, shape the dough into a loaf and let it rest for 30 minutes. Preheat your oven to 450°F and bake for about 30 minutes until golden brown.

Classic Carrot Cake

An homage to tradition and familial bonds, enriched with the natural sweetness of carrots and a crunch of walnuts. This cake holds a special place in Denzel's heart as it was his late grandfather's favorite.

×
  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 2 cups granulated sugar
  3. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  5. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. 1 cup vegetable oil
  7. 4 large eggs
  8. 2 cups grated carrots
  9. 1 cup crushed pineapple
  10. 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
×

First, preheat the oven to 350°F. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Stir in oil and eggs until smooth.

Result:

Fold in the carrots, pineapple, and walnuts, then pour the batter into a greased baking pan. Bake for 30-35 minutes.

Result:

Allow the cake to cool before frosting with cream cheese frosting.

Tips
&
Tricks

  • Measure ingredients accurately.
  • Allow eggs and butter to reach room temperature.
  • Rotate the baking tray halfway through the baking process for an even bake.
